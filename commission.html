Commission
a painting

A commissioned painting is a chance to create something made entirely for you. Tell me about a feeling, a colour palette, a space — and I'll translate that into an original work, painted with the same intention as everything in my studio.

1

Get in touch

Fill in the form with your ideas — size, mood, colours, budget. The more you share, the better. I'll get back to you within 1–2 days to see if we're a good fit.

2

We agree on the details

Once we've talked through the brief, I'll confirm the price and timeline. A 50% deposit is required before I begin.

3

I paint

I work from your brief in my Berlin studio. Commissions typically take 2–4 weeks from the start date depending on the size and medium.

4

You approve

Before shipping, I send you a photo of the finished work. Once you're happy, the remaining balance is due and I arrange delivery.

5

Delivered to your door

I ship worldwide. Shipping costs are quoted individually based on size and destination.
