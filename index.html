About

Benedicte Ambjerg is a Danish artist living and working in Berlin. Her figurative paintings explore the emotional lives of women — vulnerability, inner strength, and the quiet tension between the two.

Her work is characterised by soft yet dark tones, restrained colour, and an intimate atmosphere that invites the viewer to pause and feel.

Original works are available for purchase. Benedicte is open to commissions — please get in touch to discuss.
