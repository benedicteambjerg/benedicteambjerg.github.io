About

I am a Danish artist living and working in Berlin. My paintings center on emotional depth and the subtle tension between vulnerability and strength, often expressed through female figures.

My work is marked by soft yet dark tones, restrained color, and an intimate, contemplative atmosphere.

Original works are available for purchase. I am open to commissions — please get in touch to discuss.
